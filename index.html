Who we are!!!

Benito hotel is a fast food resturant located in central business centre of poland along side oxford towers . We take pride in ensuring our guests enjoy the most relaxing and comfortable stay in tastefully furnished rooms and suites offering spectacular ocean, pool and garden viewsFor dining and entertainment, guests can choose from three themed superb restaurants, two great bars, and the exclusive Cocos Beach Bar, overlooking the beautiful Montana beach. There are four outdoor swimming pools with water slides, spring board, diving boards and four floodlit tennis courts that also serve as a landing ground for helicopters. Special offers are given on a discount of 25 percent for lunch dinner for a table of 5 people
